Broccolini with Lemon Oil

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Lemon
  • One-third cup of Olive Oil
  • 3 pounds of Broccolini, trimmed and thick stalks halved lengthwise
  • Garnish with Lemon Wedges

Preparation

1. Remove zest from lemon in 1 inch-wide strips with a vegetable peeler. Halve lemon and set aside.

2. Heat oil and zest in a 5 quart pot over moderate heat until zest is golden. Remove from heat and discard zest, reserving oil in pot.

3. Cook broccolini in an 8 quart stock pot until crisp tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain.

4. Reheat lemon oil over medium to high heat. Then add broccolini and cook, tossing, until heated through, about 1 minute.

5. Add salt and pepper to taste, then squeeze half a lemon over broccolini.
